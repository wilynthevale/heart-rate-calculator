Heart Rate Zones

Zone Descriptions

  • Zone 1 (50-60%): Recovery, easy pace.
  • Zone 2 (60-70%): Endurance, comfortable pace.
  • Zone 3 (70-80%): Aerobic, moderate effort.
  • Zone 4 (80-90%): Threshold, hard effort.
  • Zone 5 (90-100%): Max effort, sprinting.
